Crescent Restaurant in Anna Nagar, Chennai, is a well-regarded dining establishment offering a range of Indian and continental dishes in a casual yet inviting atmosphere. Open daily from morning until midnight, it caters to both lunch and dinner crowds, with extended hours on weekends. The restaurant is praised for its consistent food quality, prompt service, and clean ambiance. Reviewers frequently highlight flavorful biryanis, appetizing starters, and reliable service as strengths. A few guests note occasional delays during peak hours and mention that seating can get crowded. Overall, it maintains a solid reputation for being a dependable dining option in the area
